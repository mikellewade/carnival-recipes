Sarah's Pasta with Cauliflower and Walnuts
------------------------------------------
Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:
1 pinch red pepper flakes
1 medium onion
1 pound whole-wheat pasta
2 heads cauliflower
Extra-virgin olive oil
